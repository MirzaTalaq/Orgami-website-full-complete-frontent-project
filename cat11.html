Identify the Folded Model

Before creating the wings, ensure that you've progressed through the initial folding steps that lead to the point where the wings are formed. This might be in models like birds, butterflies, or other creatures.Ensure the model is positioned correctly, following the instructions up to the step where the wings are to be created. The paper might resemble a body or a central form with specific sections representing the future wings.
