Fold the Flaps Outward

Depending on the instructions, take the designated flaps and fold them outward. The folds are usually made away from the centerline of the model, allowing the wings to extend outward from the body. Ensure that both sides of the model are symmetrical by folding both flaps or wings equally. This symmetry is crucial for the overall appearance of the finished model.Confirm that the resulting wings align with the expected outcome as per the instructions. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure the wings are balanced, symmetrical, and reflect the intended design.
