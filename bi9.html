Selecting the Paper

Origami paper is typically square-shaped and comes in various sizes and colors. You can either purchase pre-cut origami paper or create a square from standard paper. If you're using standard paper Take a rectangular sheet of paper and fold one corner diagonally across to meet the opposite edge. Ensure the edges align perfectly to form a triangle. Crease the fold well, then trim off the excess paper along the edge of the triangle. Unfold to reveal a square.
